eLA's Student Information System (IrisSIS&trade;) is a web-based platform facilitating different types of tasks and services related to registration, profiling, enrollment, curriculum management, grades, reporting as well as many other services. <br />
IrisSIS&trade; meets accountability demands for the desired performance and attainment.
